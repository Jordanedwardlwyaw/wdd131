Mountain Trails Explorer

Site Name

Mountain Trails Explorer is a site dedicated to hikers and outdoor enthusiasts who want to discover, share, and plan their hiking adventures on mountain trails worldwide.

Domain availability (optional): mountaintrailsxplorer.com

Site Purpose

The site provides detailed trail maps, user reviews, weather updates, and hiking tips. It also includes a community forum and a planning tool to create custom hiking routes.

User Scenarios

  • Where can I find beginner-friendly mountain trails near my location?
  • How can I share my recent hiking photos and reviews with the community?

Color Schema

The primary and secondary colors chosen reflect a natural, outdoor theme:

  • Primary Color: #2a5d34 (Dark Green) – Used for all main headings and accents to evoke nature.
  • Secondary Color: #f0e6d2 (Light Beige) – Used as the page background to create a soft, natural feel.
  • Accent Color: #d9534f (Red) – Used sparingly for highlights, buttons, and important notices.

Typography

  • Georgia (serif): Used for headings to give a classic, readable style.
  • Segoe UI (sans-serif): Used for body text to ensure clarity and modern appearance.
  • Courier New (monospace): Used for code snippets or special accents, like wireframe labels.
