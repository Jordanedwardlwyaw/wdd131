About Kampala

Kampala, the capital city of Uganda, is vibrant, lively, and full of culture. From bustling markets to scenic hills, it’s a city that never fails to impress visitors.

Top Attractions

Gaddafi National Mosque

A stunning mosque atop Kampala hill offering panoramic views.

Uganda Museum

Discover Uganda's rich cultural heritage and history.

Ndere Cultural Centre

Enjoy traditional music, dance, and crafts from Uganda.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: June–August & December–February.
  • Currency: Ugandan Shilling (UGX).
  • Local transport: Boda bodas (motorcycle taxis) and matatus (minibuses).
